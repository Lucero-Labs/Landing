Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Lucero Labs Credential Wallet is a verifiable credential manager that helps you securely store and manage your digital credentials on your mobile device. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Our app does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. All credentials you store are managed entirely on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We collect minimal technical information to maintain and improve the app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data when the app crashes to help us fix bugs
  • Usage Analytics: Basic anonymized data about app features used (no personal information or credential data)
Important: We never collect, store, or transmit your credentials, personal documents, or any information contained within your credentials to our servers.

2. Camera and QR Code Scanner

Our app uses your device camera exclusively for:

  • Scanning QR codes to receive new credentials
  • Scanning QR codes to present credentials to verifiers

What we don't do:

  • We do not record, store, or transmit photos or videos from your camera
  • We do not use your camera for any purpose other than QR code scanning
  • Camera access is only active when you explicitly choose to scan a QR code

3. Biometric Authentication

Our app uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition, etc.) to secure access to your credentials.

Important details:

  • All biometric data is processed and stored exclusively by your device's operating system
  • We never access, collect, store, or transmit your biometric information
  • Biometric authentication is optional and controlled by your device settings

4. How Your Data is Stored

All your credentials and data are stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information:

  • Credentials are encrypted and stored in your device's secure storage
  • No credentials or personal data are transmitted to Lucero Labs servers
  • No credentials or personal data are stored in the cloud
  • You have complete control over your data

5. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information.

The only time information leaves your device is when:

  • You choose to present a credential: When you scan a verifier's QR code and explicitly confirm, the requested credential information is shared directly with that verifier
  • Anonymous analytics: Minimal, anonymized technical data may be shared with analytics providers (no personal or credential data)

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children
  • The app does not require account creation or personal information
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • Parents can review and delete all app data by uninstalling the app

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your credentials are accessible within the app at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual credentials or all data within the app settings
  • Export: You can back up your credentials as permitted by the app
  • Camera Permission: You can revoke camera access in your device settings (this will prevent QR scanning)
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data

8. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All credentials are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Biometric authentication adds an extra layer of protection
  • Local-only storage means your data never travels over the internet unless you explicitly share a credential
  • Regular security updates and patches

9. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services for minimal analytics and crash reporting:

  • These services only receive anonymous, aggregated technical data
  • No personal information or credential data is shared with third parties
  • These services help us improve app stability and performance

10. International Users

Our app is available globally. Since all data is stored locally on your device, international data transfer regulations do not apply to credential storage. Any minimal analytics data collected is processed in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. Legal Compliance

We comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European users
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for users under 13
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
